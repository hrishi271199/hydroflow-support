Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to HydroFlow. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Only: HydroFlow stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

The app stores locally:

  • Water intake entries (amount and timestamp)
  • Dehydration factor entries (if using Premium features)
  • App preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your hydration history and statistics
  • Calculate daily hydration goals
  • Provide insights into your hydration patterns
  • Deliver the app's core functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure data storage frameworks (SwiftData). We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Apple In-App Purchases: If you purchase a Premium subscription, Apple processes the transaction. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment information. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings (if enabled in development mode)
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Children's Privacy

HydroFlow is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
